Von's Raspberry Chocolate Cake

Home

Ingredients:

Cake:

  • Vegan Margarine (for greasing)
  • 21⁄3 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1⁄2 cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon Salt
  • 11⁄2 cups Granulated Sugar
  • 11⁄2 cups Soy Milk
  • 1⁄2 cup Canola Oil
  • 1⁄2 cup Raspberry Preserves, warmed and strained.
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Frosting:

  • 3 tablespoons Soy Milk
  • 3 ounces Vegan Semisweet Chocolate, broken into small pieces
  • 1⁄2 cup Confectioners' Sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Maple Syrup

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 9-inch cake pan and line with parchment paper.
  2. Sift flour, cocoa, baking powder, and baking soda into a large mixing bowl and stir in salt and sugar.
  3. Pour soy mik into a medium saucepan and add the oil, raspberry preserves, and vanilla extract. Place over medium heat and whisk to combine. Stir into the dry ingredients and mix thoroughly.
  4. Transfer to the cake pan and bake for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Let cool on a wire rack.
  5. To make frosting, heat the soy milk in a small saucepan over medium heat until it boils. Add chocolate pieces and stir until melted. Remove from heat and whisk in confectioners' sugar and maple syrup. Set aside to cool before frosting the cake.
