Shakshuka (Eggs Poached in Tomato Sauce)

Home

Description

Eggs poached in tomato sauce! You can leave out the hot peppers and harissa if you can't handle spice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • One 28oz. can of whole tomatoes
  • One large onion
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • One large bell pepper (or any other sweet pepper!)
  • One hot pepper
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp harissa
  • 6 eggs

Directions

  1. Cut onion in half and cut again into thin half moons. Bring the onions to a medium-low pan with the olive oil and saute, stirring constantly until carmelized, 30 to 45 minutes.
    • I'm serious don't skimp on this I promise it's worth it.
  2. Dice the peppers and add them to the pan. Bring the heat up to medium-high. Cook for another 10 minutes.
  3. Add minced garlic, spices, and harissa. Cook until fragrant, 30 seconds.
  4. Add canned tomatoes. Use a wooden spoon to crush up the tomatoes. Let the sauce reduce until thickened, About 45 minutes. Stir every 15 minutes.
  5. Once the sauce is ready, season to taste and turn the heat off. Use the back of a spoon to make wells to crack the eggs into. Season the eggs with salt and pepper and cover the pan with a lid. Cook under low heat for 5-8 minutes.
  6. Serve with toasted bread
